Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new product?

To add a new product, navigate to the 'Products' section and click on 'Add Product'. Fill in the required details and click 'Save'.

How can I track my orders?

You can track your orders in the 'Orders' section. Here, you will find the status of each order and detailed information about each order.

How do I contact support?

To contact support, click on the 'Contact Support' button at the bottom of this page, or send an email to support@example.com.

How can I update my account information?

To update your account information, go to the 'Account Settings' section and edit your details. Make sure to save your changes.
